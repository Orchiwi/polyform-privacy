Polyform — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 21 May 2026

Short version: Polyform keeps your game data on your device only. If you choose to join the online leaderboard, your chosen username and score are stored on our server. Ads are shown by Google AdMob. We do not track you, sell your data, or share it with anyone other than Google for ad delivery.

1. Who we are

Polyform is an indie mobile puzzle game developed and published by an independent developer. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at orchiwideveloper@outlook.com.

2. Information we collect

2a. Data stored only on your device

The following information is saved locally using your device's standard storage (Android SharedPreferences). It never leaves your device unless you opt into the online leaderboard (see 2b).

  • Best score
  • Settings: sound on/off, haptic feedback on/off, dark mode on/off, language preference
  • Your chosen leaderboard username (pseudo)
  • Coin balance and experience points (XP)
  • Achievement unlock status
  • Daily challenge progress and completion state
  • Time-attack best time
  • Owned color palettes (cosmetic items)

This data is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app. We cannot access it remotely.

2b. Optional online leaderboard

If you enter a username (pseudo) in the settings, Polyform offers the option to submit your best score to a global leaderboard. This is entirely optional and opt-in. No score is submitted if you leave the username field empty.

When a score is submitted, the following data is sent to and stored on our server:

  • Username (pseudo) — a 3-to-16-character alphanumeric name you choose yourself
  • Score — your best game score
  • IP address — collected automatically by the server at the time of submission (used for basic abuse prevention)
  • Timestamp — date and time of the submission

Please choose a username that does not reveal personal information. We recommend not using your real name.

2c. Advertising data (Google AdMob)

Polyform shows ads delivered by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads in accordance with Google's advertising policies.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, the app will display a consent prompt (required by GDPR) the first time you launch it. If you decline personalized ads, only non-personalized ads will be shown. Your consent choice can be reset in the app settings.

3. How we use your information

  • Local game data: solely to save your progress and preferences across sessions.
  • Leaderboard data: to display a ranked list of scores to all players. Your username and score are visible to the public on the in-app leaderboard.
  • IP address: to detect and prevent automated spam or abuse of the leaderboard. It is not shared publicly.
  • Ads: Google AdMob uses data to display relevant advertisements. We do not receive personal information from Google as part of this process.

We do not use your data for profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. The only third party that receives data related to your use of Polyform is Google AdMob, for ad delivery purposes. No other analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services are used in this version of the app.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including all EEA residents under GDPR), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights:

  • Device-local data: uninstall the app. All locally stored data is permanently removed.
  • Leaderboard data: email us at orchiwideveloper@outlook.com with your username (pseudo) and we will permanently delete your entry from our server.
  • Ad personalization: use the consent reset option in Settings, or adjust your device-level ad ID settings.

6. Data retention

  • Local data: retained until you uninstall the app.
  • Leaderboard data (username, score, IP, timestamp): retained indefinitely to keep the leaderboard accurate, unless you request deletion by contacting us.
  • Ad-related data: governed by Google's own retention policies.

7. International data transfers

Our leaderboard server is operated via Supabase on infrastructure located in the European Union (eu-west-1 region). If you access the leaderboard from outside the EU, your data travels to and is stored within the EU.

Google AdMob may transfer and process data in countries outside your own, including the United States, in accordance with Google's privacy policies and applicable data transfer mechanisms.

8. Children's privacy

Polyform is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted information through the leaderboard feature, please contact us at orchiwideveloper@outlook.com and we will promptly delete that information.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the data stored on our server (including HTTPS encryption for all network communication, access controls on the database, and regular security reviews). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Local data on your device is protected by Android's application sandboxing model — other apps cannot access Polyform's local storage.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. If the changes are significant, we will notify you within the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of Polyform after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
